Professional Summary

Technical Program Manager with over twelve years of hands-on experience running complex, high-stakes programs at the intersection of technology and healthcare. The work has largely lived inside care delivery and health system environments where the stakes are real — slow programs affect patients, not just project timelines. Extensive experience working across Clinical, Engineering, Product, and Operations teams on programs that do not come with clean requirements or simple answers: EHR integrations, telehealth platform buildouts, virtual care workflow redesigns, and enterprise-scale digital transformation efforts in regulated environments.


What stands out is the ability to turn ambiguity into structure without slowing teams down — building roadmaps that engineering teams can actually execute, communicating program status in a way that gives executives what they need to make decisions, and holding cross-functional groups accountable in a way that keeps working relationships intact. Most of this career has been spent in fast-moving environments where priorities shift and systems are still being figured out — that is where the best work happens.
